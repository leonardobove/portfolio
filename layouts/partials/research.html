Qubase — Python Pulse Sequencer for FPGA Quantum Control

A high-level Python pulse sequencer built on top of the QICK FPGA platform. Qubase models pulse sequences as directed acyclic graphs (using rustworkx), enabling symbolic scheduling, parallel branch execution, and automated calibration workflows. Applied to characterize 2D and 3D superconducting qubits at Fermilab and support advanced quantum experiments including parallel fast-decay detection sequences.

QPCB — Quantum Pulse Conversion Board

Designed and analyzed a custom microwave PCB for pulse frequency conversion in quantum computing setups. Full electromagnetic simulation using ADS Keysight, including S-parameter optimization and signal integrity analysis. The board bridges FPGA output frequencies to qubit drive frequencies, reducing system complexity.
